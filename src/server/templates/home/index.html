Hifumi is a bot that will get all your moderators on the same page

Moderation

Hifumi remembers things about your members for you!


Ideally you want to know who everyone is in your server at all times but keeping track of all your members becomes harder and harder as your server grows.


Note things about users for a later time.

Warn or strike users anonymously without exposing your mods.


Once a user is past 3 (or a number of your choice) strikes, they're out.


Warnings and strikes (infractions) expire automatically after 2 weeks.

Someone breaking the rules? Check their history to see if another other moderator acted or had anything to say previously.


No point warning someone for the 10th time.


This site is still under heavy construction, the API will be the main focus until Hifumi goes public.
